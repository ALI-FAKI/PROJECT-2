Welcome to Coral Health Tanzania

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting Tanzania's vibrant marine ecosystems. Our mission is to preserve the country's precious coral reefs for future generations through community engagement, education, and direct conservation efforts.

Our Ethos

We believe in the power of community and education to drive lasting change. Our work is centered on three core principles: **sustainability**, **collaboration**, and **respect for nature**.

What We Do

Our core activities include **coral reef monitoring**, **hands-on restoration projects**, and **community education programs**. We work to restore damaged reefs and ensure their long-term health and survival.

Where We Work

We are focused on key coral reef sites along the coast of Tanzania. Our projects are implemented in close collaboration with local communities, ensuring our efforts are sustainable and meet local needs.
